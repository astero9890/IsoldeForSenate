Isolde von Dittarsdorf
Serving Member of Parliament for Ambrosia

Born in Strawberry, Ambrosia to a family of musicians and educators, Isolde Margarethe von Dittarsdorf entered politics with a focus on practical governance, regional development, and building a more stable and resilient Caprica.

"To maintain a graceful posture, be like a bird. Chin up, chest out, but not too much. Stay balanced and straighten your legs. You see, the books I used to carry on my head are long gone, but their weight is still with me."
