POLICIES YOU CARE ABOUT
National Defense

Caprica's national defence is no longer limited to our borders alone, but also the protection of the infrastructure and systems that keep our country running day by day. I support strengthening our national resilience through modern cybersecurity standards, improved emergency preparedness, and protecting critical sectors such as energy, transportation, healthcare, and communications from emerging threats. A secure and prepared Caprica is essential to maintaining stability, public confidence, and national security in the modern age.
The Economy

A strong economy must be built on responsibility, stability, and long-term growth rather than short-term political thinking. I support responsible budgeting that keeps Caprica financially sustainable while continuing to invest in the infrastructure, regional development, and industries that support jobs and economic opportunity across the country. Economic growth should not only benefit major cities, but also the communities and regions that keep Caprica moving forward every day.
Infrastructure

Reliable infrastructure is essential to keeping Caprica connected, competitive, and economically strong. I support continued investment in transportation, regional connectivity, and public infrastructure that improves access between our communities, supports local economies, and prepares our country for future growth. From roads and rail to modern logistics and regional development, infrastructure should serve every part of Caprica, not just our largest cities.
Stability & Governance

Stable and effective governance is essential to maintaining public trust and ensuring that Caprica's institutions can function reliably in times of both calm and crisis. I believe in strengthening the capacity of government to make decisions efficiently, uphold accountability, and avoid unnecessary deadlock that slows progress. By improving cooperation across institutions and focusing on practical, solutions-driven leadership, we can ensure that Caprica remains stable, responsive, and capable of delivering results for its citizens.
Rural Development

Rural and regional communities are a vital part of Caprica's identity and economy, and they deserve the same opportunities for growth and prosperity as our major cities. I support targeted investment in regional infrastructure, agricultural support, and local services to ensure that rural areas are not left behind. Strengthening these communities is not only a matter of fairness, but also essential to building a more balanced and resilient national economy.
